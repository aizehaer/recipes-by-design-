Chicken fried steak is a southern tradtion, prapered with grandma's hand and enjoyed with a good old sweet tea. When u bite throu the crunchy layer, the warm gravy will meet the tender grand beef in the middle. And that is where the magic happens. Ingredients

  • 1 pound pre-tenderized cube steaks
  • Salt
  • eggs, beaten
  • milk
  • flour
  • garlic powder
Cooking steps
  1. Pound the steak thin
  2. Press the flour into the meat
  3. Spoon hot oil over the steaks in the pan
  4. Use a warming oven
